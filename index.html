Hello, It’s Kaizonaro

Gabriel Zonaro a.k.a. Kaizonaro is a Brazilian music producer and Beatboxer. He started DJing and producing for hobbie around 2015, inspired by his cousin who introduced him to the world of PsyTrance at the age of six. In 2017 began his career as a beatboxer and joined the group BeatFellas - Beatbox Nacional - BR. Today, with more than 15 battles won, Kaizonaro seeks to merge elements of Trance, Techno, Brazilian Funk, Dubstep, Trap and Metal into their songs and live performances of beatbox.




Kaizonaro is also:
  • Guitarrist at Project Nephilim (with Strakten before, now with TranZe and ToAtoa)
  • Beatboxer at BeatFellas - Beatbox Nacional - BR


Old Projects
  • Guitarrist at OniSan (with Strakten, left after 2019)
